Open Data Mesh Platform

An open source XOps platform that manages the full lifecycle of a data product from creation and validation, through deployment and access management, to decommissioning. It is released under Apache 2.0 license. Contributions from the data community are welcomed!

ODMP is also part of Blindata, which actively contributes to its development.

Get started Check the Platform

Open

ODMP is released under Apache 2.0 license. You can use the platform as it is or extend it according to your needs. You can also join our community and actively participate in its evolution providing feedback or even becoming a committer.

Standard-driven

ODMP's modular structure relies on open specifications and protocols to optimize interoperability with other systems. Adherence to existing standards is vital to create a supportive ecosystem of mesh tools that are easily integrated and adaptable to changes over time.

Self-serve

ODMP has been designed by data engineers, for data engineers. Its primary objective is to streamline and simplify the various data-ops activities related to managing the lifecycle of a data product. The platform’s modules offer self-service capabilities, allowing data teams to efficiently validate, create, deploy, monitor, and evolve their data products, as well as manage access to them.

Extensible

ODMP is technology agnostic. You can effortlessly expand it by using adapters to incorporate your preferable tools for essential features such as provisioning, CICD, metadata management, and enforcing policies, among others. With ODMP, it's all about accommodating your preferences seamlessly, enabling you to take advantage of your past investments.
